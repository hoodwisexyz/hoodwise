01 / Foundations

What is Robinhood Chain?

An Ethereum-compatible, permissionless L2 for onchain financial infrastructure. ETH pays gas; permissionless access does not mean every Robinhood product is universally available.

02 / Stock Tokens

Stock Tokens explained

They are tokenized debt securities providing economic exposure, not direct equity ownership. Always verify the canonical contract and your eligibility.

03 / Earn

Robinhood Earn

A variable-yield, onchain lending product for eligible users—not a bank account and not FDIC insured.

04 / Verification

Verify a contract

Use the exact address, check canonical status, source verification, owner controls, holders, and the relevant DEX pool—not a ticker alone.

05 / Community tokens

Memecoin & launchpad safety

Community launchpads are discovery surfaces, not endorsements. Research the exact contract, liquidity, ownership controls, and current conditions.
