CONTRACT SAFETY

Verify the contract before trusting the ticker.

Use the exact address, not a ticker or social post. Hoodwise can surface canonical status, source verification, explorer activity, and indexed pools.

WHAT IS CONFIRMED

Start with the exact address

Check whether it is a contract, whether it matches a canonical asset, and whether Blockscout has verified source code.

WHAT TO VERIFY

Then inspect risk signals

Review owner controls, holders, pool liquidity, and current conditions. These checks reduce uncertainty; they never prove safety.
