STOCK TOKENS

Economic exposure, not equity ownership.

Robinhood Stock Tokens are tokenized debt securities designed to provide economic exposure to an underlying stock. They are not direct shares in the issuer.

WHAT IS CONFIRMED

They are distinct from shares

Holding a Stock Token does not grant legal or beneficial ownership, voting rights, or ordinary shareholder status in the underlying company.

WHAT TO VERIFY

The exact contract

A ticker is not enough. Verify the canonical address in official Robinhood Chain documentation and confirm jurisdiction/product eligibility separately.
