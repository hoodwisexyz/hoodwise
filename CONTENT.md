# Hoodwise content kit

Short-form X posts that route readers to [Hoodwise](https://hoodwise.xyz). Adapt the tone, but keep the factual boundaries intact.

## 1. Robinhood Chain in one post

Robinhood Chain is a permissionless Ethereum L2 built for onchain financial infrastructure. ETH pays gas. It is EVM-compatible. Anyone can deploy, but product availability and eligibility still vary. Hoodwise separates the chain facts from the product claims: https://hoodwise.xyz

## 2. Stock Token distinction

A Robinhood Stock Token is not the underlying share. It is a tokenized debt security designed to provide economic exposure. The exact contract matters: a matching ticker at another address is not canonical. Verify before interacting: https://hoodwise.xyz/app

## 3. Community token checklist

Before touching a Robinhood Chain community token: get the exact contract, check verified source/owner controls, inspect holders, find the specific pool, and check current liquidity. A name, launchpad listing, or chart is not verification. DYOR: https://hoodwise.xyz/app

## 4. NOXA / launchpad framing

Launchpads are discovery surfaces, not endorsements. A community token can be interesting to research without becoming an official Robinhood asset. Hoodwise helps separate the platform, the contract, the pool, and the unknowns: https://hoodwise.xyz/app

## 5. Contract intelligence CTA

Paste a Robinhood Chain contract into Hoodwise. It can surface canonical status, Blockscout source verification, proxy type, explorer activity, and indexed DEX pools when available. Read-only research, no wallet connection: https://hoodwise.xyz/app

## Content guardrails

- Do not call a community token official, safe, or guaranteed.
- Do not publish a contract address without linking the source/explorer.
- Do not make price predictions or personal trade calls.
- Keep DYOR compact and concrete: contract, liquidity, owner controls, market conditions.
